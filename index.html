A Tribute to My little Ones

“A daughter is just a little girl who grows up to be your best friend.”

In the vast expanse of my heart, where emptiness once echoed, two radiant stars appeared. They danced into my life, filling every corner with their melodies, their laughter, and their boundless love. These two precious souls, Lyric Arabella and Kailani Ember, transformed my existence into a symphony of joy.

Lyric Arabella: The Melody Within Lyric Arabella, my firstborn, arrived as a sweet refrain. Even before her tiny feet touched the earth, she swayed to the rhythm of music—my music. In the quietude of my womb, she listened to the melodies that flowed through my veins. Her kicks were harmonies, her hiccups gentle notes. I knew then that her name must reflect this connection—a lyrical bond that transcended mere words.

Lyric Arabella, with her almond eyes and a heart as vast as the universe, taught me the language of motherhood. We waltzed through sleepless nights and sun-kissed mornings. She hummed lullabies to the moon, and I sang along, our voices blending into a celestial chorus. Her laughter, like wind chimes in a summer breeze, echoed through our home.

In her presence, I discovered the magic of simplicity—the way a dandelion could be a wish, a cardboard box a castle, and raindrops a symphony. She reveled in life’s smallest details, finding wonder in dew-kissed petals and butterfly wings. Together, we composed verses of love, etching them into the fabric of time.

Kailani Ember: The Fiery Spark And then, like a comet streaking across the night sky, Kailani Ember burst forth. She arrived with a roar—a tempest of energy and fire. Her cries were thunderstorms, her laughter crackling lightning. Sleep became a distant memory as she explored the world with insatiable curiosity. Kailani was a force of nature, unyielding and fierce.

Her eyes held galaxies—the promise of adventure, the thrill of discovery. She raced through life, leaving footprints in mud puddles and laughter in her wake. Kailani taught me that chaos could be beautiful, that messiness was a canvas waiting for imagination to paint upon. She was my hurricane, my whirlwind, and I reveled in the storm.

Kailani Ember, with her wild hair and a spirit untamed, reminded me that love need not be tranquil. It could be a tempest, a wildfire, a symphony played at full volume. She challenged me to dance in the rain, to chase after rainbows, and to embrace every shade of emotion. Her hugs were hurricanes, wrapping me in warmth and wonder.

A Life Transformed Together, Lyric and Kailani compose the soundtrack of my days. Their laughter intertwines with the rustle of leaves, their dreams echo in the night sky. They are my heartbeats, my constellations, my purpose. Each day, they gift me new verses—sometimes tender, sometimes exuberant—but always filled with love.

As the sun rises and sets, I watch Lyric’s gentle melodies blend with Kailani’s fiery crescendos. They harmonize, clash, and create a symphony uniquely theirs. And in this grand composition, I find my purpose—the reason my heart expanded to accommodate their brilliance.

Lyric Arabella and Kailani Ember, my daughters, my muses, my life’s greatest masterpiece. You are the lyrics to my song, the colors in my palette, the stars that guide me through the night. Thank you for filling my days with purpose, my nights with wonder, and my heart with boundless love.

With love and gratitude,

Your forever melody-maker
